Privacy Policy - JeuxGratis

Data Collected

The JeuxGratis application collects the following data:

  • Player name (entered by the user)
  • Game score (Yams)

Data Storage

Data is stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud service).

No Tracking

We do not collect location, advertising identifiers, or biometric data.

User Rights

Leaderboard data is public. Account deletion is not currently available (beta version).
